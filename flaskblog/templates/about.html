About Me

This is what I do.

Welcome to my blog! My name is Heni (you can call me Hanaw), and I'm so glad you're here. I created this website as a platform to share my thoughts, experiences, and expertise on topics related to my passion.

I'm an energy engineer with 10 years of experience and I love programming especially building websites. Throughout my career, I've developed a passion for problem-solving by designing and auditing many installation in many industries. I believe that by sharing my knowledge and insights with others, I can help them navigate the challenges and opportunities in this field.

But this blog isn't just about my professional experiences. In my free time, I love to build web applications, learning something new about astrophysics, watching my favorite team FC BARCELONA, playing some DOTA. I believe that these personal pursuits help to inform and enrich my work, and I hope that they can provide inspiration to my readers as well.

Whether you're here to learn something new, gain a fresh perspective, or simply connect with a like-minded individual, I hope that you'll find something of value on this blog. Thank you for stopping by!
